Product Vision

A home that
understands you.

Not one you have to program.

AURA (Adaptive Unified Responsive Automation) is a screen-less, voice, and sensor-first AI home system. It observes, adapts, predicts, and acts—learning your lifestyle automatically without manual programming or complex apps.

Busy Professionals

Want deep automation and comfort without the burden of managing or programming it.

Families

Require multi-user personalization, safety monitoring, and intuitive controls for kids.

Elderly Users

Benefit immensely from zero-interaction control, predictive maintenance, and passive security.

Routine Learning

No Manual Setup

The system observes daily behaviour over 1-2 weeks and automatically builds personalised home routines. No "If/Then" programming required.

Example Scenario

You wake at 7 AM on weekdays. AURA learns this: it automatically turns on soft lights, warms the bathroom floor, and skips the routine on weekends.

How AI Works

Machine Learning detects patterns via clustering algorithms. Anomaly detection identifies schedule changes, and continuous retraining ensures the system evolves with you.

Personalisation Per User

Multi-User Ready

AURA recognises each family member through voice, UWB identity, or motion patterns, and applies their specific preferences automatically.

Example Scenario

Dad enters the living room—temp adjusts to 22°C, evening news plays. A child enters later—a soft nightlight activates, and parental audio controls enforce.

How AI Works

Voice modeling and UWB beaconing verify identity locally. Collaborative filtering applies historically preferred states to the specific user.

Security Monitoring

24/7 Passive Guard

The system establishes a normal behavior baseline. It flags anything unusual—odd entry times, unknown presence, or spatial anomalies.

Example Scenario

A door opens at 3 AM. AURA detects it breaks the baseline, instantly alerts the owner, turns on all perimeter lights, and logs the event.

How AI Works

Predictive behavioral modeling establishes a baseline. Advanced anomaly detection and presence pattern analysis flag deviations instantly.

Contextual Voice + Control

No Wake Words

Natural speech understanding paired with contextual awareness. No robotic wake words, no exact syntax needed. Fully multilingual.

Example Scenario

Just say "It's freezing in here" out loud, and the heat increases. Say "Goodnight" while in bed, and AURA turns off lights and activates sleep mode.

How AI Works

Local Natural Language Processing (NLP) models combined with sentiment analysis to understand intent rather than just parsing rigid commands.

UX Strategy

Human-in-the-Loop UX

AURA is designed to be a partner, not a dictator. When autonomous prediction isn't enough, users interact naturally through physical objects, body language, and ambient feedback.

Interaction without Screens

  • Tangible UIs (TUIs): Physical objects (like beautifully machined analog dials) restore satisfying tactile feedback. Perfect for children and the elderly.
  • Micro-Gestures: A simple flick of the wrist raises volume; a flat palm down turns off a room. Tracked silently by mmWave radar.
  • Ambient Feedback: AURA talks back via the environment. A soft pulse of light confirms a locked door; a subtle localized warm breeze signals the thermostat adjusting.

Learning Curve: Manual Inputs over Time

As AURA observes routines, the need for manual interactions (voice/gestures) drops to near zero.

Technical Foundation

The SLIP Architecture

To ensure AURA is accessible and scalable, all hardware and software choices follow the SLIP framework. No million-dollar retrofits required.

1. The Sensory Layer (No Cameras)

AURA replaces invasive cameras with mmWave Radar (to detect micro-movements, breathing, and posture) and UWB Anchors (for centimeter-level identity tracking). It utilizes inexpensive commodity hardware ($40 radar nodes) deployed via standard USB-C.

2. The Cognitive Layer (Local Edge AI)

All Routine Learning and Personalisation happens locally on a dedicated Edge Node (e.g., Mac Mini M4 or Raspberry Pi 5). AURA builds a Context Graph mapping temporal, spatial, and physiological data. Raw data never goes to the cloud, guaranteeing absolute privacy.

3. Interoperability & Actuation

AURA "Plays Well" by acting as an intelligent translation layer. It connects via Matter/Thread to orchestrate the existing ecosystem devices you already own (Philips Hue, Sonos, Ecobee) without requiring you to replace them.
